Katie's Blurbs

About me:


Hello! I'm Katie and I hope to be your first Junior Designer.

I have been with DigitalOcean since June 2019 as a Developer Experience Advocate. Prior to DO my professional background was largely in Marketing, however I always knew that these roles were not fulfilling my creative needs.

After graduating I landed my first remote job, which allowed me to work remotely from 51 different countries across 6 continents. I spent 5 of those years living in Paris and hope to move back one day in the future.

While traveling, I learned 4 new languages. This helped me connect with locals in a more meaningful way and led to some of my best memories. I have always been fascinated by the human experience and how we all see the world so differently, I feel that this curiosity would be an asset to the role.

In my free time you can find me painting, writing code, or wandering through a museum.

What I'd bring to your team:

  • I know our product in and out. My current role requires a fair amount of technical knowledge and cross-collaboration. I have built good relationships by working with nearly every team in the company.
  • I'm here to dream big and innovate.
  • I'm a hobbyist programmer and understand both our technical limitations and how to best work with developers.
